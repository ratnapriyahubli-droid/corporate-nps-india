  • At retirement under current NPS rules, at least 40% of the corpus must be used to purchase an annuity; up to 60% can be withdrawn tax-free as a lump sum. Annuity income is taxable in the year of receipt.
  • Tax savings calculations assume the NPS contribution comes over and above your fixed CTC (a typical Corporate NPS arrangement). Confirm your actual structure with HR/Payroll.
  • No personally identifiable information is captured, stored, or transmitted by this calculator — all computation happens locally in your browser.
